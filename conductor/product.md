# Product Guide: FitQuest

## Core Vision
FitQuest is a gamified fitness application that transforms the often tiring routine of physical exercise into a continuous, epic RPG journey. By establishing a physical-digital bridge, the app maps real-world effort (PASSOS, CALORIAS, gym visits) into virtual Recompensas, effectively solving the problem of workout evasion through immediate feedback and progression.

## Target Audience
The primary audience for FitQuest is **casual exercisers who struggle with consistency and need RPG-style motivation**. It aims to turn the daunting task of forming a habit into an engaging game.

## Key Mechanics & Progression
1. **The Hero's March & Activities:** Utilizing device sensors for daily PASSOS, as well as manually logged diverse activities (e.g., cycling, swimming, yoga), users compute evolution points to progress.
2. **Herói Progression:** Users will level up a personal Herói and unlock cosmetic gear as they burn CALORIAS and complete activities.
3. **Masmorra Exploration:** Real-world gym check-ins (validated via Google Places API) are interpreted as exploring new "Masmorras" or adventure arenas.
4. **Daily missoes:** Users will engage in daily missoes driven by step counts and calorie burn to maintain daily engagement.
5. **Chefe Battles:** Time-limited challenges where users must accumulate a specific amount of physical activity (converted into damage) within a timeframe to defeat Chefees and earn Recompensas.
6. **Achievements & Titles:** Users are Recompensaed with badges and custom titles for their profile as they reach significant milestones (e.g., total PASSOS, Chefees defeated), driving long-term engagement.
7. **Adaptive Class System:** Users are dynamically assigned an RPG class (e.g., Warrior, Ranger) based on their real-world fitness habits and achievements. Each class grants specific XP modifiers to Recompensa consistency in their preferred activities.

## Social & Interaction
FitQuest is designed as a **strictly single-player journey for personal motivation**, focusing entirely on the individual's progress and connection with their Herói without the pressure of competitive leaderboards.
